SAMPLE CLINICAL NOTES FOR TESTING medReport
============================================

--- SAMPLE 1: COPD ---
65-year-old male with 30 pack-year smoking history presenting with chronic productive
cough for the past 3 years, progressive dyspnea on exertion, and wheezing. Patient
denies fever or chills. Spirometry shows FEV1/FVC ratio of 0.62. Chest X-ray reveals
hyperinflation and flattened diaphragm. No signs of pneumonia.

--- SAMPLE 2: GERD ---
42-year-old female presenting with persistent heartburn and acid regurgitation,
particularly after meals and at night. Reports burning sensation in chest and sour
taste in mouth. No chest pain radiating to arm. Symptoms worsen with spicy foods
and coffee. Endoscopy shows mild esophageal erosion. No dysphagia noted.

--- SAMPLE 3: DIABETIC NEUROPATHY ---
58-year-old male with 15-year history of Type 2 diabetes, poorly controlled.
HbA1c at 9.2%. Patient reports numbness and tingling in bilateral feet and lower
legs with burning pain at night. Denies chest pain or shortness of breath.
Monofilament test shows reduced sensation. Glucose remains elevated despite Metformin.

--- SAMPLE 4: HYPERTENSION ---
52-year-old male presenting for routine checkup. Blood pressure recorded at 158/96
mmHg on three separate occasions. Reports occasional headaches and dizziness.
Family history of cardiovascular disease. No edema. ECG normal. BMI 29.
Lifestyle modifications advised previously with poor compliance.

--- SAMPLE 5: MIGRAINE ---
28-year-old female with recurrent throbbing headache, predominantly unilateral,
associated with nausea, vomiting, and photophobia. Aura present before onset.
Frequency: 3-4 episodes per month, each lasting 6-24 hours. Denies fever or
neck stiffness. No neurological deficits on exam. MRI brain unremarkable.
